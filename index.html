About Us

A Community Built on Faith & Service

The Islamic Center of New Rochelle (ICON) is a vibrant mosque serving the Muslim community of Westchester County and the surrounding New York area.

We offer daily prayers, Jumu'ah services, Quran classes, and community programs for all ages. Our doors are open to visitors, neighbors, and anyone seeking to learn more about Islam.

Rated 4.9 ★ on Google with over 131 reviews, ICON is a trusted spiritual home for hundreds of families in the region.
